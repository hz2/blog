AI in Programming

Using AI assistants for software development
December 7, 2024 · 1 min · 118 words · jd | Suggest Changes

Recently, it has been difficult to go a day without seeing use of the words: AI, LLMs, GPT, Copilot, Claude, etc. I want to provide a perspective for those who are considering using one of the many AI-assistant programming aids.

Be wary if you are learning or if the AI/LLM is learning

There’s no doubt that AI can be used as an aid in solving difficult problems, but as I just finished my time in University and see increasing headlines related to the use of LLMs in programming, I worry about young developers. It seems too easy to use AI in your studies or personal-learning that I think would take away the value of learning the material yourself.
